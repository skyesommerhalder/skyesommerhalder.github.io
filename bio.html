Bio

Hello world, I am Skye Sommerhalder! I am currently attending Sheridan College's Honour’s Bachelor of Film and Television Program. I aspire to become a successful writer/producer within the G.T.A film and television industry. My specialties are in the fields of production and writing. Throughout my school career I have spent the past three years of my four-year Honour’s B.A with nominations and/or wins at every single Sheridan Screen Arts Award Show. When not at work I enjoy going for walks (usually on trails so I can enjoy the scenery), reading, video games, movies, photography, dancing, and indie/alternative music.
